My Story

I spent 16 years at Volusion, one of the original ecommerce platforms, where I wore nearly every hat: first web developer, senior developer, manager, solutions engineer, and product lead. I've seen what works (and what doesn't) at every stage of a business's online journey.

Over the years, I worked directly with hundreds of businesses — from solo entrepreneurs launching their first store to established brands doing millions in annual revenue. I optimized checkout flows, debugged performance bottlenecks, migrated complex catalogs, and trained teams on everything from SEO fundamentals to custom integrations.

Now, I'm using that experience to help small businesses cut through the noise. Most sites don't need a complete rebuild — they need someone who knows where to look, what to fix, and how to prioritize. That's what I do.

I combine 16 years of hands-on development expertise with modern AI tools to deliver fast, effective solutions. Whether it's boosting your Lighthouse score, fixing critical SEO issues, or building a custom site from scratch, I focus on what actually moves the needle for your business.
